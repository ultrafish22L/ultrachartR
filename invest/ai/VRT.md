## Overview

Vertiv designs, manufactures, and services critical digital infrastructure for data centers — power management, thermal management (cooling), and IT infrastructure. The company is the default integrated solution for hyperscalers deploying gigawatts of AI compute capacity, with deep integration with Nvidia. Every AI GPU needs power and cooling, and Vertiv provides both.

## Bull Case

- $15B backlog provides years of revenue visibility — doubled in recent quarters
- Liquid cooling demand is accelerating as GPU power density increases
- Margin expansion: adjusted operating profit growing faster (33%) than revenue (23%)
- AI CapEx cycle is multi-year — every data center build benefits Vertiv
- 22 Buy ratings vs 0 Sells — broad analyst consensus
- Picks-and-shovels play with less technology risk than chip companies

## Bear Case

- $93B market cap on $10B revenue = ~9x P/S — not cheap, PE of 72x is elevated
- Power/cooling is lower-margin than chips — harder to justify premium valuation
- Competition from Schneider Electric, Eaton, and other industrial players
- Supply chain constraints on specialty cooling components
- Revenue growth (23%) is strong but lower than semiconductor peers

## Key Risks

- Hyperscaler CapEx slowdown would directly impact Vertiv's order book
- Valuation multiple has expanded significantly — priced for perfection
- Competition intensifying as more industrial companies enter AI cooling
- Supply chain execution on $15B backlog
- Currency and macro risk as a global industrial company

## Summary

Vertiv is the best pure-play on AI data center infrastructure — the power and cooling layer that every GPU requires. The $15B doubled backlog is arguably the clearest forward-looking indicator of sustained AI infrastructure demand. With deep Nvidia integration and leadership in liquid cooling, Vertiv is positioned to benefit from every dollar of AI CapEx regardless of which chip company wins. The risk is that at ~9x P/S and 72x PE, the market already prices in strong execution on that backlog.
