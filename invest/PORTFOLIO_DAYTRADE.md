## Trading Rules

1. **Position sizing:** $500–$1,500 per trade (10–30% of capital). Never >40% in one trade.
2. **Max risk per trade:** 2% of account ($100). Use stop losses.
3. **Max daily loss:** 5% of account ($250). Stop trading for the day if hit.
4. **Take profits:** Don't get greedy. Scale out at +3%, +5%, +10% levels.
5. **No overnight on leveraged/inverse ETFs** unless a deliberate swing.
6. **Options rules:**
   - No selling naked calls/puts (unlimited risk on $5K is stupid)
   - Buy calls/puts for directional bets — define max loss upfront
   - Spreads allowed to reduce cost basis
   - Never let options expire worthless if they still have value — close before expiry
7. **Journal every trade** — entry reason, exit reason, what was learned.
8. **Earnings plays:** Size down (max $500 risk) — binary events are coin flips.
