## The Inflation Hedge Angle

In inflationary environments, consumer staples outperform because:
1. **Inelastic demand** — people buy food, cleaning products, beverages regardless
2. **Brand pricing power** — Tide, Coca-Cola, Doritos can raise prices
3. **Dividend growth** — many are Dividend Aristocrats/Kings (50+ years of increases)
4. **Franchised models** — companies like MCD have pricing flexibility + scale
