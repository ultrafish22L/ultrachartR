## Why Gold Now

Gold is the single best hedge for the current macro environment:
- DXY at 4-year low (~97), forecast to drop to 94 by Q2
- JP Morgan targets $5,055–$6,300/oz by end 2026
- Fed cutting rates toward 3.0–3.25% (weaker dollar = stronger gold)
- Central banks buying gold at record pace (de-dollarization)
- Geopolitical risk elevated (Iran, tariffs, trade uncertainty)
- $1.9T fiscal deficit, 101% debt/GDP — debasement fears

## Top Picks

### GDX — VanEck Gold Miners ETF
- **Why:** Broad basket of gold miners. +155% performance. Low-effort diversified exposure.
- **Use case:** Core gold allocation in long-term portfolio.

### NEM — Newmont Corporation
- **Why:** World's largest gold producer. Only gold miner in S&P 500. +167%.
- **Market Cap:** Large-cap, institutional quality.
- **Risk:** Operational complexity, multiple mines globally.

### AEM — Agnico Eagle Mines
- **Why:** Premium operator, strong execution, production beating guidance. +116%.
- **Risk:** Concentrated in Canada (political risk lower but weather risk).

### B — Barrick Mining (fka Barrick Gold)
- **Why:** #2 producer globally. +182%. Diversifying beyond gold into copper.
- **Risk:** Name change to "B" reflects diversification — less pure gold play.
