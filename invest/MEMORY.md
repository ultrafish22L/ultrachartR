## User Timezone

- Market hours: 4:30 AM – 11:00 AM HST
- Typical check-in: ~11:00 AM–12:00 PM HST (post-close)
- Simulate day trades at open/close prices; journal on check-in
- **Automated scheduled tasks (Windows Task Scheduler):**
  - `InvestPreMarket` — 4:00 AM HST daily → invest/scripts/premarket.bat → premarket_report.md
  - `InvestPostClose` — 11:30 AM HST daily → invest/scripts/postclose.bat → postclose_report.md
  - Reports saved to invest/scripts/ — user reviews when convenient
  - Note: requires PC to be awake and user logged in (Interactive mode)
  - Manage: `schtasks.exe //query //tn "InvestPreMarket"` or `"InvestPostClose"`
  - Delete: `schtasks.exe //delete //tn "InvestPreMarket" //f`
