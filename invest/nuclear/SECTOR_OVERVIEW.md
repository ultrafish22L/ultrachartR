## Why Nuclear Now

Nuclear is at the intersection of two mega-trends:
1. **AI data center power demand** — each GW-scale data center needs baseload power that only nuclear can reliably provide
2. **Government commitment** — $80B+ U.S. funding for new reactor deployment (Cameco/Brookfield/Westinghouse partnership)
3. **Supply deficit** — World Nuclear Association says 4x increase in uranium production needed to fuel planned reactor tripling
4. **65 reactors under construction** globally as of late 2025
5. **Bipartisan support** — rare area of political agreement
