- **Action plan:** Full cash deployment into quality at generational prices.

## The Meta-Strategy

**We're playing two games simultaneously:**

1. **The AI secular trend** — this is real, multi-year, and our core (45%). We hold through volatility because the fundamentals (revenue, CapEx, demand) support the thesis. We DON'T chase, we DON'T panic sell, we buy dips.

2. **The macro hedge game** — dollar weakness, inflation, potential correction. Our hedges (gold, international, uranium, copper, staples, bitcoin) should GAIN when AI stocks dip. This is the whole point of diversification — when one sleeve zigs, the other zags.

**The portfolio should NEVER go to zero on any single scenario.** In a crash, AI drops but gold/cash/staples hold. In an AI boom, tech carries but hedges underperform (acceptable). In dollar collapse, gold/international/bitcoin surge and offset tech weakness.

**The day trading portfolio is the aggressive playground.** Take the shots here. Learn. Build pattern recognition. The $5K is tuition money — if we lose it all, the $100K portfolio is untouched.
