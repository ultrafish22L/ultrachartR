## Why Healthcare Now

A "Great Rotation" is underway (Feb 2026):
- Institutional capital flowing out of overextended tech into healthcare
- Healthcare, utilities, and staples ALL outperforming S&P 500 YTD (as of Feb 5)
- Health care is inherently defensive — people don't skip prescriptions in recessions
- Aging demographics = structural demand growth
- Biotech valuations have been depressed for years — rotation is unlocking value
